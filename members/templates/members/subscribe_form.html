Become a member

Great that you're interested in becoming a member of Quadrivium, the student music association! If you're not sure yet, please fill in the other form. The yearly contribution fee for a membership is around 75 euros for students and 150 for non-students. Please note: Your membership will be automatically extended. If you wish to unsubscribe, you must inform the secretary.

Why we need these personal details is indicated in our privacy document (in Dutch).
